Governance Framework

Community Governance Model

HyperFlow Protocol implements a fair governance system where every FLOW token grants equal voting power, ensuring community representation without plutocratic concentration of power.

  • Proposal Threshold: 10,000 FLOW minimum to submit governance proposals
  • Voting Period: 7-day voting window for all proposals
  • Quorum Requirements: Minimum 5% of circulating supply must participate
  • Implementation Delay: 48-hour timelock before execution

Economic Incentives & Deflationary Mechanisms

Supply Management

  • Fee Burn Mechanism: Optional quarterly token burns from protocol revenue

  • Buyback Programs: Protocol treasury repurchases during high revenue periods
