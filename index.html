Token Economics & Scarcity Mechanisms

Fixed Supply Management

1,000,000,000 FLOW tokens with no inflation or additional token creation, ensuring long-term scarcity and value preservation.

Quarterly Fee Burns

Community governance can vote to burn tokens from protocol revenue, reducing circulating supply and increasing scarcity over time.

Extended staking periods reduce circulating supply while providing enhanced rewards and governance participation benefits.

Utility-Driven Demand

Increasing protocol usage drives continuous token demand for governance, fee discounts, yield enhancement, and ecosystem access.
